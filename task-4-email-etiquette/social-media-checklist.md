# Responsible Social Media Use – Checklist

| ✅ Do’s | ❌ Don’ts |
|-------|---------|
| Use strong and unique passwords | Share personal or sensitive information publicly |
| Verify information before sharing | Spread fake news or unverified content |
| Maintain respectful and professional behavior | Post offensive or inappropriate content |
| Adjust privacy settings regularly | Ignore privacy and security settings |
| Use social media for learning and networking | Waste excessive time affecting studies |

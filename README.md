# Digital Literacy & Cybercrime Awareness Project

## Overview
This project is designed as part of my role as a **Student Digital Ambassador**. The goal is to raise awareness about digital literacy, professional online presence, safe online practices, coding collaboration platforms, email etiquette, and cybercrime prevention. Each module focuses on practical skills, awareness, and resources that help students stay safe, responsible, and professional online.

---

## Module 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
**Objective:** Help batchmates understand what digital literacy is and why it matters.  
- **Tool used:** Canva  
- **Content:** The infographic covers:
  1. What digital literacy is
  2. Useful digital tools for students
  3. Safe internet practices  
- **Interesting/Difficult point:** I found it interesting to visually represent the concept of digital literacy in a clear, easy-to-read format while keeping it simple and engaging.

## Module 5 – Cybercrime Awareness & Prevention
**Objective:** Help peers recognize and avoid online threats.  

**Part A – Case Study:**  
- **Type:** Cyberbullying  
- **Victim:** Amanda Todd (real-life case)  
- **Content:** Describes what cyberbullying is, how it happens step-by-step, who is targeted, and consequences (emotional distress, isolation, and tragic loss)  
  

**Part B – Stay Safe Online Checklist:**  
- 8 actionable tips for college students, including 2 UPI/financial safety tips  
- Includes reporting channels: National Cyber Crime Portal ([cybercrime.gov.in](https://www.cybercrime.gov.in)) and Helpline 1930  


**Reflection:** I was surprised how easily cybercrime can target anyone online. I plan to personally change the habit of sharing sensitive info online and always verify links or contacts before interacting.
